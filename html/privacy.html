1. Definitions

In this agreement, "the Company," "we," "us," or "our" refers to STAR INVESTMENT (ZANZIBAR) LIMITED, a professional investment and consultancy firm registered under the laws of Zanzibar.

"Services" refers to the range of financial consultancy, institutional brokerage, and asset management advisory provided through our digital platforms and physical offices.

"User" or "Client" refers to any individual, corporate entity, or legal representative accessing our services or website.

2. Scope of Services

STAR INVESTMENT (ZANZIBAR) LIMITED provides high-tier advisory services specifically tailored for Zanzibar's emerging markets. Our services include but are not limited to:

  • Strategic investment planning for international institutional clients.
  • Real estate and infrastructure development consultancy.
  • Cross-border transaction facilitation and regulatory guidance.
  • Bespoke market research and due diligence reports.
Note: All financial services are subject to jurisdictional availability and specific client eligibility criteria as mandated by international financial regulations.
